Welcome to Silamalas

Surprise your loved ones and book the "Silamalas" recreation complex! 🥂

Because what could be more valuable than moments when you see your family truly happy, peaceful, and together? 😇

Imagine... You are sitting in a hot tub, surrounded by silence and the sounds of nature, with your closest people next to you, your favorite drink in hand, and the sunset slowly fading in the sky... This is not a dream — it could be your next weekend at "Silamalas". 💫

🥰 "Silamalas" is a place to forget worries, regain strength, and simply enjoy life.

✨ What awaits you:

  • A hot tub, a cozy sauna, and a pool for cooling off
  • A roof terrace with peaceful nature views for unhurried conversations
  • A spacious outdoor terrace with an equipped kitchen and a relaxation area by the fireplace
  • A campfire spot with rocking chairs – a place where the warmest memories are born
  • Diverse activities for the whole family: volleyball, basketball, trampoline, novuss, badminton, Zorb balls, mini ping-pong, and much more

🏕️ "Silamalas" isn't just a place to stay... it's a feeling. It's freedom. It's your well-deserved break from the daily routine.

🌿 Established in 2015, the "Silamalas" recreation complex is located in picturesque Milzkalne – in a quiet forest enclosure, at the top of a hill, where nature itself creates a special atmosphere. Here you can truly disconnect from the rush – warm up in the sauna, enjoy the hot tub, and refresh yourself in the pool. In the evenings, watch the sunset with a glass of champagne or hot chocolate, surrounded by the glow of the fireplace.

📍 Convenient and advantageous location: only 60 km from Riga, just a 10-minute drive from the Milzkalns ski track, and only 10 km from the sea – perfect for both active recreation and peaceful moments in nature.

🤍 To ensure your stay is truly special, peaceful, and undisturbed, we rent the complex to only one group at a time – everything is exclusively for you and your loved ones.

🎉 "Silamalas" is an ideal place for various events – birthdays, corporate events, retreats, camps, weddings, and other special occasions.
The complex offers 26 sleeping places arranged in 6 separate rooms, and we also provide tent spots for larger groups. Our spacious 0.7 ha territory can comfortably host up to 80 guests.

✨ One reservation – and unforgettable memories for the whole family, friends, or colleagues.

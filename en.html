About the "Silamalas" recreation complex

Peaceful rest surrounded by nature

Established in 2015, the "Silamalas" recreation complex is located in picturesque Milzkalne – in a quiet forest enclosure, at the top of a hill. It is a place to regain strength, forget daily worries, and fully relax from the everyday rush.

The complex is conveniently located – 60 km from Riga, a 10-minute drive from the "Milzkalns" ski track, and 10 km from the sea. To ensure maximum privacy and peace, the complex is rented out to only one group of clients at a time.

A suitable place for any event

"Silamalas" is a suitable place for various events – birthday celebrations, corporate events, retreats, camps, and weddings.

For the convenience of guests, 26 sleeping places are available, spread across 6 separate rooms. Tent spots are also offered for larger groups. The spacious 0.7-hectare territory can comfortably accommodate up to 80 guests.

Summer season: Equipment included in the price

The price includes everything necessary for full recreation and entertainment:

  • Comfortable sleeping places, a pool, volleyball and basketball courts, as well as a cozy campfire spot.
  • A spacious outdoor terrace with a fully equipped kitchen and a lounge area by the fireplace (firewood for about 3 hours is included).
  • A roof terrace with a beautiful view, swings, and a 5-meter trampoline.
  • Sports equipment and games: badminton, novuss, and mini ping-pong.
  • Charcoal grill with skewers and grates for preparing meals.
  • Complex infrastructure: 4 toilets, 1 shower, and free WiFi throughout the territory.
